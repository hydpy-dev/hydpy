HydPy-W (WALRUS)¶

The HydPy-W model family is based on the WALRUS model, specifically designed to simulate surface water fluxes in lowland catchments influenced by near-surface groundwater [3].

Base model:

  • wland (W-Land)

Application model:

  • wland_v001 (semi-distributed WALRUS model)
    • Integration tests
      • base scenario
      • seepage
      • surface water supply
      • snowfall
      • No vadose zone
      • No land area
  • wland_v002 (experimental)
    • Integration tests
      • base scenario
      • seepage
      • surface water supply
      • snowfall
      • No vadose zone
      • No land area
