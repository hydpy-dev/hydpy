HydPy-W (WALRUS)¶

The HydPy-W model family is based on the WALRUS model, specifically designed to simulate surface water fluxes in lowland catchments influenced by near-surface groundwater (Brauer et al., 2014).

Base model:

  • wland (W-Land)

Application model:

  • wland_v001 (semi-distributed WALRUS model)
  • wland_v002 (experimental)
