HydPy-W (WALRUS)¶

The HydPy-W model family is based on the WALRUS model, specifically designed to simulate surface water fluxes in lowland catchments influenced by near-surface groundwater (Brauer et al., 2014).

Available models:

  • wland » HydPy-W (base model)
  • wland_wag » HydPy-W-Wag (extended version of the original Wageningen WALRUS model)
  • wland_gd » HydPy-W-GD (extended version of the WALRUS model with modified groundwater dynamics)
