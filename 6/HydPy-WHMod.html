HydPy-WHMod¶

The HydPy-WHMod model family provides hydrological models for analysing and predicting various aspects of the water balance at specific sites and larger areas. It focusss on SVAT-like descriptions of vertical processes. The basic model concept was initially developed by Probst (2002).

Base model:

  • whmod » HydPy-WHMod (base model)
  • whmod_rural » HydPy-WHMod-Rural (WHMod for rural regions)
  • whmod_urban » HydPy-WHMod-Urban (WHMod for urban regions)
